## Counselling

I am an accredited integrative counsellor offering individual and couple therapy in London Bridge and remotely by video and phone. Counselling can give you a safe space to explore the difficult and painful elements of your life to try and make more sense of them and work towards change. Whatever troubles you are facing, I will try and support you, without judgement, to reach a place where life feels more settled and meaningful. I work to a strict code of ethics and abide by the BACP's Ethical Framework, for whom I am an accredited and registered member. I have over 10 years' experience of working with a variety of services, including the NHS, Mind and Rethink and people from all different backgrounds.

## Mediation

Mediation provides a confidential non judgemental space for those who may be in conflict to express themsleves openly. As an accredited mediator with UK Mediation and with over 10 years experience as a counsellor, I have the relational experience and skills to support individuals to understand one another better and work together to come to a mutual agreement as to how to move forward. I understand the pressures and demands of working or living alongside others and I can bring a calm, steady presence to help build trust and progress towards solutions.

## Reflective practice

Reflective pratice is an opportunity for teams and individuals to step back from their work and reflect upon it in a safe, confidential way. In pressurised environments, teams suffer from not taking time to take a pause and talk through difficult times. I help facilitate safe spaces for teams to meet with one another, take stock and think about the impact of their work and how they wish to progress.
