## Counselling

I am an accredited integrative counsellor offering individual and couple therapy in London Bridge and remotely by video and phone. Counselling can give you a safe space to explore the difficult and painful elements of your life to try and make more sense of them and work towards change. Whatever troubles you are facing, I will try and support you, without judgement, to reach a place where life feels more settled and meaningful. I work to a strict code of ethics and abide by the BACP's Ethical Framework, for whom I am an accredited and registered member. I have over 10 years' experience of working with a variety of services, including the NHS, Mind and Rethink and people from all different backgrounds.

We would meet at the same time each week. Sessions are 50 minutes long. I do not charge for missed sessions provided you give at least a week's notice.

**Fees: £70-90**
